How to Join

Become a Digital Leader and help shape the future of technology at West Island School.

Who can apply?

We welcome motivated students from all year groups who share a passion for technology, helping others, and making a difference at WIS. No prior technical experience is required — just enthusiasm and a willingness to learn.

  • Open to all WIS students
  • All skill levels welcome
  • A passion for technology and helping others
  • Commitment to attend regular sessions

What to expect

As a Digital Leader you'll join one of our specialist teams, attend weekly meetings, and contribute to real projects that benefit the whole school.

  • Choose a team: Design, Research, Code, or Management
  • Work on meaningful school-wide projects
  • Develop real digital and leadership skills
  • Present work at showcases and assemblies

How to apply

Applications open at the start of each academic year. Fill in the online form, tell us which team interests you and why you'd make a great Digital Leader.

  • Look out for announcements at the start of term
  • Complete the online application form
  • Attend a short informal interview
  • Receive your team placement
