Our Mission

Enable smarter, safer, and more inclusive use of technology across West Island School.

What we stand for

Digital Leaders exists to bridge the gap between students and technology. We believe every learner deserves access to powerful digital tools — and the skills to use them responsibly.

  • Empowerment: Give every student the confidence to use technology effectively.
  • Responsibility: Promote safe, ethical, and privacy-conscious digital habits.
  • Inclusion: Ensure no student is left behind in the digital age.
  • Innovation: Encourage creative, forward-thinking approaches to learning and problem-solving.

Our goals

  • Provide reliable peer-to-peer tech support across all year groups.
  • Run engaging, practical workshops on coding, design, and digital safety.
  • Collaborate with staff to integrate technology meaningfully into lessons.
  • Share knowledge, skills, and enthusiasm for all things digital.
