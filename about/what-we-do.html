What We Do

From peer support to coding workshops, Digital Leaders make technology accessible for everyone at WIS.

Peer Support

Our support team runs regular drop-in sessions where students and staff can get hands-on help with devices, apps, and school systems. No problem is too small — we're here to help everyone feel confident with technology.

  • Device troubleshooting and setup
  • App guidance and workflow tips
  • Password and account help
  • 1-to-1 tech mentoring

Workshops

We design and deliver engaging workshops throughout the year, covering a wide range of digital skills for all experience levels.

  • Coding fundamentals (Python, JavaScript)
  • Web design with HTML, CSS, and frameworks
  • Automation and productivity tools
  • Digital safety and online privacy
  • UI/UX design and prototyping

Initiatives

Digital Leaders go beyond the classroom to drive meaningful technology projects across the school community.

  • Annual school hackathon
  • AI and ethics discussion panels
  • Digital wellbeing campaigns
  • Collaborative research projects
  • End-of-year showcase events

Working with staff

We partner with teachers and school IT to integrate digital tools meaningfully into lessons and school operations. Our leaders act as bridges between the student body and the wider school technology strategy.
