Meet the Team

Our student-led teams work together to support, design, research, and build technology solutions across WIS.

  • 🏛️ Digital Leaders
    • 🏛️ Management
      • 🎨 Design
      • 🔬 Research
      • 💻 Code
🏛️

Management

The Management branch oversees all Digital Leaders projects and events. They coordinate cross-team collaboration, set strategic goals, and ensure every branch has the resources and direction needed to succeed.
🎨

Design

The Design branch creates visuals, layouts, and user experiences that make technology engaging and accessible. From branding and logos to UI/UX prototypes, they shape how the school community sees and interacts with our work.
🔬

Research

The Research branch explores emerging technologies, automation tools, and digital-literacy initiatives. They investigate new ideas, evaluate software solutions, and share their findings to help the wider school community stay informed.
💻

Code

The Code branch builds and maintains the digital tools and websites used by Digital Leaders and WIS. They work across frontend, backend, and automation, turning ideas from other branches into real, working technology solutions.
